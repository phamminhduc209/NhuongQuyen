The temporary staffing industry is on track to generate a projected $150 billion in North America annually. Express Employment Professionals reached $3.4 billion in sales in 2017 and has been ranked the No.1 staffing franchise in the Entrepreneur 500 for seven years running! Our start-up offices average more than $1 million in sales in their first year, with mature offices averaging $6.46 million annually.* Plus, as an Express franchise owner, you control your life with flexible weekday hours in a professional business setting. Express is a growing franchise with more than 800 locations and a 35 year history of success.
